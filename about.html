Empco is a renowned company specializing in the sale of Ayurvedic and Siddha medicines. Founded by Mr. K Elangovan, Empco herbal aims to provide high-quality products and promote the accessibility of ancient medicinal systems worldwide. With over 60 certified Siddha-Ayurveda products adhering to GMP and AYUSH standards, Empco ensures that its formulations meet the highest quality requirements. These products are designed to effectively address a wide range of prevalent diseases.

In addition to its product line, Empco operates Siddha-Ayurveda clinics staffed by experienced and qualified doctors. These clinics prioritize not only disease treatment but also the overall well-being of patients, providing holistic healthcare services. Empco's goal is to offer excellent care that encompasses all aspects of patients' health.

Empco further extends its services through panchakarma units, which provide holistic healing options. These units, available alongside Empco clinics, offer diverse treatments aimed at detoxification, stress relief, and rejuvenation. Moreover, Empco plans to establish dedicated health centers solely focused on panchakarma therapies, yoga, naturopathy, and other fitness and healing practices. These centers will serve as comprehensive solutions to cater to all the health needs of patients.

By combining the production of high-quality traditional medicines, the operation of Siddha-Ayurveda clinics, and the establishment of specialized panchakarma units and health centers, Empco aims to promote and make accessible the numerous benefits of ancient medicinal practices. Their vision is to ensure that individuals worldwide can benefit from these time-honored systems of medicine, fostering well-being and holistic healing.
