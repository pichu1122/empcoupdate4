PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

 Orthoraksha Tablets are made from the choicest of Classical Ingredients. The uniqueness of Orthoraksha is that it does NOT contain shallaki or guggulu. Along with providing relief from Pain, they also make sure that they do not cause any undesirable side-effects.
